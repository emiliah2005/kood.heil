About RUJA

RUJA is a legendary Estonian ensemble that has made significant contributions to the music scene in Estonia. Formed in the 1980s, RUJA's unique blend of music has touched the hearts of many with its captivating melodies and thought-provoking lyrics.

"RUJA's music is a reflection of the Estonian spirit, combining tradition with modernity."

Music

Explore RUJA's discography, featuring a wide range of songs and albums that have left a lasting impact on Estonian music culture. From rock to folk, their music has transcended genres and inspired generations.
